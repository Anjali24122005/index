About Us

+NineOne is a dynamic and rapidly growing real estate and hospitality group based in Kanpur. Built upon a 40+ year legacy in hospitality, +nineone started in 2020 to pursue real estate opportunities within Logistics. We have since delivered over 1.2 million square feet of warehousing parks with another 2.1 million square feet under construction — doubling AUM each year (currently at Rs. 625 Crores) — in just our Logistics arm. Our family background is in the hospitality business, including The Landmark Towers, Kanpur’s only five-star hotel, Status Club Resort, Kingston Resort, and standalone restaurants. In 2022, we ventured into Goa and now have two award-winning restaurants – HOSA and Fireback, operated by EHV International (the group which owns Indian Accent and Comorin). Plus-NineOne is expanding to hospitality and logistics opportunities across India, alongside investing in companies within technology and real-estate that empower our existing businesses to embrace a future-forward mindset.
